# Keplore Expertize

A human-AI co-working platform — think Slack, but built from the ground up around human-AI collaboration.

## Vision

Most AI integrations bolt an AI assistant onto an existing workflow. Keplore Expertize inverts this: AI is a first-class participant, not a plugin. Humans and AI agents work together in shared workspaces — discussing, drafting, deciding, and shipping — side by side.

## Core Concepts

- **Shared workspaces** — Channels and threads where humans and AI agents co-exist as peers
- **AI as a co-worker** — AI participants can be assigned tasks, contribute to discussions, and take action without constant prompting
- **Transparent collaboration** — Every AI action is visible, auditable, and overridable by humans
- **Async-first** — Designed for teams that work across time zones, with AI filling gaps when humans are offline
